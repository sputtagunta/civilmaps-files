## Comparative Analysis: Sravan vs. Stefan

### **Press Activity**
- **Sravan Era**: 42+ articles over 6.5 years = ~6.5 articles/year average
  - Peak activity: 2016-2018 (product launches, fundraising)
  - Stealth mode: 2012-2015 (4 articles total)

- **Stefan Era**: 17+ articles over 3 years = ~5.7 articles/year average
  - More strategic, focused communications
  - European market emphasis
  - Quieter period during strategic review (2021-2022)

### **Strategic Focus**
- **Sravan Era (2012-2019)**:
  - Product development and innovation
  - Fundraising (Seed + Series A)
  - Technology demonstrations and awards
  - U.S. market focus
  - Academic: Medium technical articles, HackerNoon deep-dives
  - Peak press during funding rounds and product launches

- **Stefan Era (2019-2022)**:
  - Geographic expansion (Europe)
  - Strategic partnerships (University of Luxembourg)
  - Commercialization and revenue focus
  - Exit strategy and M&A
  - More business-focused vs. technical communications
  - Press focused on expansion and partnerships

### **Press Tone Shift**
- **Sravan Era**: Technical innovation, "world's first," breakthrough technology
- **Stefan Era**: Market expansion, partnerships, commercialization, strategic positioning

7. **Content shift**: Technical/innovation press (Sravan) → Business/expansion press (Stefan)
